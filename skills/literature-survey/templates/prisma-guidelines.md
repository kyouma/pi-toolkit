## Inclusion / Exclusion Criteria

**Inclusion criteria:**
- Published in peer-reviewed venue or established preprint server (arXiv)
- Proposes or evaluates a method relevant to [your topic]
- Reports quantitative results on a relevant dataset or benchmark

**Exclusion criteria:**
- Not written in English (unless language-diverse search applies)
- Does not include sufficient methodological detail to assess reproducibility
- Duplicate publication of the same method/results
